Welcome

This page collects common information about the venue, registration, and travel information for the following scientific events taking place in Frankfurt in September 2018:

PPDP 2018

September 3-5, 2018

The 20th International Symposium on Principles and Practice of Declarative Programming

LOPSTR 2018

September 4-6, 2018

The 28th International Symposium on Logic-based Program Synthesis and Transformation

WFLP 2018

September 6, 2018

The 26th International Workshop on Functional and Logic Programming

The events will take place at Goethe-University, Campus Westend, 60323 Frankfurt am Main, Germany.

More information will be made available on this site as it becomes available.
